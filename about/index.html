About

I am a computer scientist with 5+ years of experience. I have a bachelors in computer science and astronomy from Williams College. I'm interested in software applications in solar physics, including machine learning. Currently, I'm working on building the data reduction pipelines for PUNCH and for CubIXSS.

I am certified in:

  • Prefect
  • NASA Open Science
  • DeepLearning.AI TensorFlow

In my spare time, I like reading, sculpting with polymer clay, journaling, and many more things.

I also have an interest in developing open source software. Check out barnsley, my tool for rendering iterated function systems.

You can reach me at hughes.jmb@gmail.com or check out my work on GitHub. You can also find me on Google Scholar.
